I am a data scientist with a background in quantitative paleoecological community analyses.

I studied invertebrate paleontology at the Richard Gilder Graduate School at the American Museum of Natural History. My research focused on understanding how periods of climate change influenced ecosystems and communities through the use of statistical and mutlivariate ordination analyses.
